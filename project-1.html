Proprio Overview

A 2D accessible game designed to improve upper limb proprioception—the sense of limb position—for stroke survivors and others with neurological impairments. Built in Unity, the app uses machine learning-based hand tracking (computer vision) to guide players in touching targets on their opposite hand, turning rehabilitation into an engaging musical game. Developed to provide cost-effective, at-home therapy with visual feedback and gamified motivation.
